THE SHANNON FAMILY LIVED IN GAYLORD, MICHIGAN, AND CLAUDE WAS BORN IN A HOSPITAL IN NEARBY PETOSKEY.
HIS FATHER, CLAUDE SR. (1862–1934) WAS A BUSINESSMAN AND FOR A WHILE, A JUDGE OF PROBATE IN GAYLORD.
HIS MOTHER, MABEL WOLF SHANNON (1890–1945), WAS A LANGUAGE TEACHER, WHO ALSO SERVED AS THE PRINCIPAL
OF GAYLORD HIGH SCHOOL. CLAUDE SR. WAS A DESCENDANT OF NEW JERSEY SETTLERS, WHILE MABEL WAS A
CHILD OF GERMAN IMMIGRANTS.
